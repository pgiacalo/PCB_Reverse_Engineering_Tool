# Component List Organized by Type

This document lists all components organized by their component type, showing designators and descriptions.

## Battery
- **B** - Battery or battery holder

## Capacitors
- **C** - General purpose capacitor
- **CE** - Electrolytic capacitor
- **CF** - Film capacitor
- **C** - Tantalum capacitor

## Diodes
- **D** - Standard diode, Schottky diode, thyristor
- **Z** - Zener diode
- **LED** - LED diode
- **IR** - Infrared diode
- **PD** - Photodiode

## Resistors
- **R** - Resistor
- **RN** - Resistor network
- **RT** - Thermistor resistor
- **VR** - Variable resistor (potentiometer / trimmer / rheostat)
- **RV** - Varistor

## Semiconductors
- **Q** - Transistor
- **U** - Integrated circuit (IC)

## Passive Components
- **L** - Inductor or coil
- **FB** - Ferrite bead
- **X** - Crystal, ceramic resonator, powered oscillator

## Power & Energy
- **B** - Battery
- **PS** - Power Supply
- **F** - Fuse

## Connectors & Switches
- **J** - Connector (jack or plug)
- **JP** - Jumper (link)
- **S** - Switch (all types, including buttons)
- **K** - Relay or contactor

## Transformers
- **TR** - Transformer

## Other Components
- **TP** - Test point
- **V** - Vacuum tube
- **LS** - Loudspeaker or buzzer
- **M** - Motor

## Generic Components
- **AT** - Attenuator or isolator component
- **CB** - Circuit breaker component
- **TC** - Thermocouple component
- **TUN** - Tuner component
